Lea Thomas

is an artist and creative coder specializing in full-stack software and website development.

Born and raised in Maui, Hawaii and a resident of New York City for over a decade, my work in music, fiber art, photography, computer programming and web development are equally inspired by both organic forms and modern functionality.
Viewing each project through an inspired lens, creative problem solving and clear communication are high priorities in my collaborative process.

As a trained full-stack software engineer with a background in the arts, I'm passionate about empowering people to express themselves and discovering their full potential by serving as a bridge between their unique visions and the virtual platform.
If you're looking for support in bringing your next great idea into the world, let's connect.
